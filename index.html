Biography

Chen Luo is a Master candidate in College of Computer Science, Jilin University, supervised by Prof. Zhe Wang and Prof. Wei Pang He received his bachelor's degree in Computer Science from Jilin University, Changchun, China, in 2012. During undergrad study, he worked as a developer intern in Sina Weibo and MIUI Group at Xiaomi During his master study, he worked as a research intern in Software Analytics Group at Microsoft Research Asia, Beijing, China. His current research interests include heterogeneous information network, data mining and machine learning.
